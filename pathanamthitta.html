Pathanamthitta


Pathanamthitta is a district located in the southern part of Kerala, India. It is known for its scenic beauty, temples, and cultural heritage. The district is surrounded by the Western Ghats on the east and the backwaters on the west. It is home to several pilgrimage centers such as the Sabarimala temple, which attracts millions of devotees every year. Pathanamthitta is also known for its rich biodiversity, and the famous Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary is located nearby. The district has a rich cultural heritage and is known for its vibrant folk arts and traditional music forms.
