Kollam


Kollam, also known as Quilon, is a district located in the southern part of Kerala, India. It is known for its historical and cultural heritage, as well as its natural beauty. The district has a long coastline, with several beautiful beaches such as the Kollam Beach, Thirumullavaram Beach, and Mahatma Gandhi Beach. The district is also home to several ancient temples, churches, and mosques, including the Kollam Ashtamudi Lake, a scenic backwater lake surrounded by coconut groves and palm trees. Kollam is also famous for its cashew nut industry, and it is one of the largest producers of cashew nuts in the country.
