Kottayam


Kottayam is a district located in the southern state of Kerala, India. It is known for its beautiful backwaters, lush green landscapes, and rubber plantations. The district is home to several notable institutions including Mahatma Gandhi University, CMS College, and the Rubber Research Institute of India. Kottayam is also famous for its rich cultural heritage, with the annual Vrischikolsavam festival at the Thirunakkara Mahadeva Temple being a major event in the district's calendar. Additionally, the district is home to several beautiful churches, including the St. Mary's Church in Bharananganam, which is a popular pilgrimage site. Kottayam is also known for its delicious cuisine, with traditional dishes like appam, stew, and fish curry being popular among locals and tourists alike.
